Banana bread

Banana bread is a cherished classic, blending ripe mashed bananas with butter, sugar, and vanilla to create a moist and flavorful loaf. With its inviting aroma and satisfying taste, it's a beloved treat for any time of day.

  • 120gr Banana
  • 60gr of oat flour
  • 20ml eggs yolks
  • 20ml almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons vainilla
  • 1 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoons of bakings soda

Steps

  1. In aa blender, incorporate all ingredients and blend until smooth
  2. In a pancake or small cake pan, previously greased with oil spray, place the mixture.
