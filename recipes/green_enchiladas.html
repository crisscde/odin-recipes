Green Enchiladas

Enchiladas are a very popular mexican dish, made with a fried tortilla base stuffed with chicken over a sauce.

Ingredients

The ingredients is for one portion

  • 5 Green tomatoes
  • Serrano chili (to taste)
  • 1 Bunch of cilantro
  • 1/2 Garlic clove
  • 120gr Turkey breast
  • 45gr Panela cheese
  • Tortillas (to taste)
  • Oil (to taste)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 onion
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps

  1. Cook the Turkey breast in water with the bay leaf, 1/4 garlic clove, 1/4 onion and salt
  2. For the sauce, we need to cook with water the green tomatoes with the serrano chili, then blend with 1/4 garlic clove, the bunch of cilantro, salt and the cooking water
  3. Add the oil to a frying pan and heat over medium heat
  4. Place each tortilla in the oil and heat for 30seg.
  5. Place the tortillas on the plate, fill them with the chicken and roll them up.
  6. Add panela cheese and enjoy your meal
